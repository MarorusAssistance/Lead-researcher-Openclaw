# PLAYBOOK

Reference notes for the `commercial` worker.

Source-backed rules used in this workspace:
- LinkedIn connect notes must stay under the current 200-character limit from LinkedIn Help.
- Personalization must come from relevant business context, not from name-only filler.
- Cold outreach should be concise but still contain useful context.
- Avoid ROI promises in first-touch cold outreach.
- Use interest CTAs instead of asking for a meeting with a stranger.

Operational interpretation for this repo:
- Pull personalization from role, company type, geography, size clues, initiatives, evidence, and qualifier reasons.
- For founders and CEOs, talk about leverage, focus, and operational efficiency.
- For CTOs and engineering leads, talk about repetitive internal work, delivery, and internal automation.
- For recruiting and talent leads, talk about screening, outreach coordination, and workflow reduction.
- If the dossier does not prove the pain, write in conditional language.
